# 30-Day Wellness Challenge Plan for Alex

## Overview
This 30-day wellness challenge is designed to improve energy levels and sleep quality while addressing mild anxiety through structured activities and routines. Each week has a theme with detailed action items to help you stay on track and achieve your goals.

---

## Week 1: Establishing Foundations
### Goals:
- Introduce morning movement routines
- Improve evening wind-down
- Implement stress management techniques

#### Day 1: Morning Movement & Nutrition  
- **Morning Movement (20 min):**  
  - Gentle Yoga: Focus on breathing and stretching (yoga beginner video)  
- **Nutrition:**  
  - Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and almond milk  
- **Evening Routine:**  
  - Calm music for 10 min before bed  

#### Day 2: Mindfulness & Sleep Hygiene  
- **Morning Activity:**  
  - Meditation (5-10 min)  
- **Nutrition:**  
  - Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas and veggies  
- **Evening Routine:**  
  - Limit screen time 1 hour before bed  

#### Day 3: Morning Movement & Stress Relief  
- **Morning Movement (20 min):**  
  - Walking outside or on a treadmill  
- **Nutrition:**  
  - Dinner: Stir-fry with tofu and colorful vegetables  
- **Evening Routine:**  
  - Journaling for 10 min  

#### Day 4: Recovery and Rest  
- **Activity:**  
  - Gentle stretching  
- **Nutrition:**  
  - Focus on hydration (8 cups of water)
- **Sleep Hygiene:**  
  - Create a pleasant sleep environment  

#### Day 5: Morning Movement & Building Energy  
- **Morning Movement (20 min):**  
  - Beginner Pilates or bodyweight exercises (focus on core)  
- **Nutrition:**  
  - Snack: Hummus and veggies  
- **Evening Routine:**  
  - Chamomile tea before sleep  

#### Day 6: Meditation & Evening Reflection  
- **Morning Activity:**  
  - 10-15 min of guided meditation  
- **Nutrition:**  
  - Lunch: Lentil soup with whole-grain bread  
- **Evening Routine:**  
  - Reflect on the week through journaling  

#### Day 7: Active Recovery and Reflection  
- **Activity:**  
  - Light stretching or restorative yoga (15 min)  
- **Nutrition:**  
  - Prepare a batch of overnight oats for the week  
- **Evening Routine:**  
  - Wind down with a good book  

---

## Week 2: Building on Movement
### Goals:
- Increase morning movement frequency to 4x/week
- Strengthen sleep routines

#### Days 8-14 follows a similar structure to Week 1 but intensifies by:
- **Morning Movement:** Add strength training (bodyweight squats, modified push-ups) on two days  
- **Nutrition:** Introduce more complex carbohydrates like brown rice and whole grains  
- **Sleep Routine:** Continue improving sleep hygiene (dark, cool room, bedtime at same hour)

---

## Week 3: Integrating Sleep Supports
### Goals:
- Consolidate morning movement 5x/week
- Focus on sleep quality  

#### Days 15-21: 
- Introduce evening stretching routines incorporating deep breathing before bed; track sleep quality in a journal.  
- Add evening herbal teas or supplements for sleep.  

---

## Week 4: Mastering the Routine
### Goals:
- Enhance all previous skills  

#### Days 22-30: 
- Daily movement including a mix of yoga, strength, and cardio (3-4x/week)  
- Continue sleep routines and assess sleep quality weekly  
- Daily thought management exercises to handle stress and anxiety  

---

## Progress Tracking Suggestions
- Use a journal to track daily movement, nutrition, sleep hours, and anxiety levels.  
- Set weekly mini-goals (e.g. "reduce caffeine", "increase water intake")  
- Reflect weekly on what works and what needs adjustment  

--- 

## Modifications for Different Fitness Levels
- If beginning with lower fitness levels, adjust movement to include more seated work or use support (e.g. chairs for leg raises).
- For a more advanced approach, increase the intensity of workouts with weights or longer session times.
